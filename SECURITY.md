At Homelab-Alpha, we prioritize the security and integrity of our repositorys. If you discover any security vulnerabilities or issues within our AdGuard Home repository, please promptly report them to Homelab-Alpha by [creating a new issue]. Your assistance in identifying and resolving potential security risks is greatly appreciated.

## Security Best Practices

In addition to the aforementioned policies, we strongly recommend adhering to the following security best practices when utilizing our repositorys:

- **Regular Updates**: Stay on top of AdGuard Home updates to patch any known vulnerabilities and ensure your installation is running the latest, most secure version.

- **Secure Configuration**: Configure AdGuard Home securely, using strong passwords and implementing appropriate access controls to limit who can access the administration interface and make changes to your settings.

- **Network Segmentation**: Consider implementing network segmentation to isolate your AdGuard Home instance from other devices and services on your network, reducing the potential impact of a security breach.

- **Firewall Rules**: Use firewall rules to restrict inbound and outbound traffic to and from your AdGuard Home instance, only allowing necessary connections and blocking any unauthorized access attempts.

- **Encryption**: Enable encryption for communication between AdGuard Home and client devices to protect sensitive data, such as DNS queries, from interception or tampering.

- **Logging and Monitoring**: Enable logging and monitoring features in AdGuard Home to track and analyze network activity, helping you identify and respond to security incidents in a timely manner.

- **Regular Backups**: Back up your AdGuard Home configuration and data regularly to ensure you can quickly recover in the event of a security incident or hardware failure.

- **Stay Informed**: Stay informed about security best practices and emerging threats in the DNS and network security landscape, so you can proactively take steps to protect your AdGuard Home installation.

By following these best practices, you can help enhance the security of your AdGuard Home deployments and minimize the risk of security breaches or unauthorized access. If you need further assistance or have specific security concerns, don't hesitate to reach out for help or guidance!
